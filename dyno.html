CVT Dyno

Design

The CVT Dyno is a project I am actively working on, but its something that I am very excited about. We design our mechanical CVT from scratch, but have limited means of controlled testing. Prior to the dyno, we would simply run acceleration times in the parking lot, which works, but is time consuming and difficult to justify if other things on the car need attention. What you see the your right is the inital packaging and frame design (which was done in Siemens NX). I would have liked to have all components on one axis, but we wanted an elevated work plane for end users and because doing so makes packaging and component swapping much easier. Tentative plan is to run a No. 50 chain between the gearbox output and the motor, which was specced according to the max power calculated at the gearbox output, with a built in FoS of 1.4.
